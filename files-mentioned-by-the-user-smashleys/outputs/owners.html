Meet The Owners

The people behind the skull logo, the hot pink, the sprinkles, and the community-first coffee counter.

Ashley & Team

Built by local people for local people.

Smashley's was created to feel different from the traditional coffee shop: more color, more personality, more energy, and more connection. Ashley and the team bring that spirit into every drink, pastry display, event table, and customer conversation.

The result is a family-owned shop that feels unmistakably Smashley's: edgy, warm, memorable, and proud to call Payson home.

Why Smashley's

A shop with a pulse.

  • Connection to Payson: A place for regulars, first-time visitors, families, and neighbors to feel seen.
  • Community involvement: Local gatherings, vendor events, seasonal celebrations, and collaborations are part of the brand.
  • Chamber of Commerce involvement: Smashley's shows up for local business relationships and community momentum.
  • Local partnerships: The shop is built to support pop-ups, makers, vendors, and community causes.
  • Business values: Be bold, be welcoming, keep it fun, and make people want to come back.
